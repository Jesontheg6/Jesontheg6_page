About Me

Born in late 1997. Jeson is currently the co-founder of GrabGas, an on demand cooking gas delivery platform. We are trying to make gas ordering fast, convenient and quick instead of the usual way of calling the gas driver via phone call.

Raised in a business family, I always wanted to start my own business ever since I was small. I bought my first business book when i was 13. At 14 i started trading my savings into Japanese Yen and China RMB to earn quick bucks. But during that time, starting a business requires a lot of money, and back then online businesses was an unheard story.

At 16, I was introduced to the tech startup scene in Malaysia through a few of highly motivated friends. Since then, i’ve been hearing stories about how college kids created an app, raised millions in funding, employed thousands of people, flew around the globe but what truly inspired me was that they changed the norm through technology and impacted millions of lives. I told myself ‘ This is who i want to be when i grow up’

Ever since that night, i decided to dedicate my life into building great technology companies with the aim of bringing humanity forward as i live by the quote “ We’re here to put a dent in the universe. Otherwise why else even be here? ”

Entrepreneurs: we grow together, we win together, Let’s change the world
